About Us

Your Trusted Fashion Partner in Bidar

Since 2013, Vijay Laxmi Matching Point has been Bidar's go-to destination for sarees, ethnic wear, and garments. We specialize in helping customers find the perfect matching combination — from sarees to blouses, from kurtas to accessories — all in one place.

Located at Kusnoor Complex, near Mohan Market, our store is known for its wide selection, friendly staff, and the trusted expertise to help you look your best for every occasion — from daily wear to festive celebrations.
